Solitaire

In this solo variant of Five Tribes, you will be confronting the Sultan Yazid who plays as a dummy player.

SET UP

  • ✦ Take the 11 blue Camels for yourself.
  • ✦ Place the 11 pink Camels next to the board. These are Yazid’s Camels.
  • ✦ Take 25 Gold Coins. (30 for the Expansion)
  • ✦ Do not use the Bid Order & Turn Order Track.
  • The rest of the set-up remains unchanged.

GAME TURN

Press Next Tile for Yazid's turn. He always goes first. He takes control of that tile, capturing all its meeples and placing his camel.
If you don’t want Yazid to clear the targeted Tile(s), you can outbid him in order to play before him.

MEEPLE EFFECT

  • Tent: For the first Red tile that Yazid takes control, use its tent and score it. You pay him.
  • Viziers: Place it next to the board. At the end of the game you lose if Yazid's Council of Viziers is larger than yours.
  • Artisans: Same effect as the Viziers.
  • Elders: Place it next to the board. At the end of Yazid's tun, if he has at least 1 Elder and 1 Fakir (or 2 Elders), he buys the first available Djinn. If he owns 6 djinns, the game is over
  • Merchants: Return them to the bag and discard as many Resource card, beginning at the start of the line, except Fakirs.
  • Builders: Do the Builders scoring action, but you pay Yazid. You lose if you run out of money.
  • Assassins: Place them next to the board. You lose if Yazid has more than 7 Assassins.

Outbid Cost: 0
